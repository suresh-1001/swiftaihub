Common questions

What offices ask before getting started.

Does it give medical or legal advice?

No. This is a hard rule built into the product. Clinical questions, legal questions, and anything requiring professional judgment are routed to your team. The AI never attempts to answer them.

How does it install on my website?

It's an embeddable widget — one script tag added to your site. No platform change, no redesign needed. We handle the configuration before it goes live.

How is it configured for my specific office?

We load it with your office's actual information — services, hours, location, fee structure, and how you want leads routed. It answers based on what's configured, not general knowledge.

Does it replace our receptionist?

No — and we don't position it that way. It covers the window when your office is closed and staff are unavailable. Human follow-up is always the end of the process.

What happens to the leads it captures?

Collected leads are sent directly to your office inbox with the visitor's name, contact details, and their request. Your team follows up the same way they would with any inquiry.
