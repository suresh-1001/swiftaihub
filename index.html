Our approach

Built different from the start.

Vertical-specific

Each tool is built for a specific office type — not a generic chatbot with a few settings changed. Dental, legal, and real estate each have different rules about what AI should and shouldn't do.

Hard limits by default

Our tools never give medical, legal, or financial advice. That guardrail is built in — not a checkbox a customer can turn off. Offices need to trust what they put on their website.

Human handoff always

Every product routes to a real person at the right moment. The AI handles intake. Your team handles everything that needs professional judgment. Clear handoff, every time.

Installs in days, not months

One script tag. Configured for your office specifically. No platform migration, no development project. Private-pilot onboarding is handled directly.
